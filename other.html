Spring 2017 to Present

As a Healthy Penn State Ambassador, I participate in a variety of campus wide events relating to health and wellness as well as inform and encourage students living on and off campus to participate in the events and free wellness services provided.

AMGA Climbing Wall Instructor Certification

I participated in an intensive, weekend-long training to become a certified climbing wall instructor in preparation for becoming a manager at Penn State's new Rock Climbing Gym.
