Community Experience

Penn State Center Philadelphia Community Nutrition Intern

May 2017 - August 2017

During this summer internship, I worked with the Penn State Center Philadelphia and six other interns to create an intern blog, Freshly Rooted in Philly, collaborated with a handful of nonprofit Philadelphia-based organizations or urban farms, and organized the 2017 Philly Urban Ag Faculty, Staff and Friends Tour, among other things. I had the privilege of sharing work space with many individuals whose goals were to improve the urban communities that they resided in. Of the many skills I gained, one that I am most proud of is workshop lesson planning and teaching.

The Common Market Nutrition Intern

May 2017 - August 2017

As a component of my Penn State Center Philadelphia summer internship, I was paired with the partner site, The Common Market. I developed handouts to be given to clients, visited many farm share pick-up sites and farm stands, I spoke with many of The Common Market's clients about food and nutrition as well as distributed nutrition education materials.

Reading Police Oral History Project

Spring 2015 and Spring 2016

As a Service Learning Leader, I collaborated with Professor Justin De Senso and his class at Penn State Berks over a course of two semesters to create a collection of mini-biographies of interviewed Reading police officers. I assisted in organizing the interviews between the officers and students, editing, and the construction of the book. I not only gained a new and valuable perspective, but I also gained editing skills and organization skills.

Penn State Health St. Joseph's - Community Benefit Report

Fall 2015

Over the course of a semester, Fall 2015, I collaborated with Dr. Kesha Morant Williams, her health communications class at Penn State Berks, CAS 253, and staff from Penn State Health St. Joseph's to create a community benefit report. The report emphasizes the impact that PSHSJ has on their community especially in terms of the programs they offer. As a Service Learning Leader, I helped with organization, developing a marketing plan, media commons procedures, etc. Some useful skills I've collected are market plan developing and filming.
