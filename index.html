Introduction

I find myself drawn to many aspects of nutrition, clinical, community, and food service management and just about everything in between. As a kid thinking about what I wanted to grow up to be, I remember always thinking that I wanted to make an impact no matter what; I really wanted to make a difference in peoples' lives. I feel that almost any genre of nutrition can help me achieve that dream.
